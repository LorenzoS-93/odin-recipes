Oreo Cheesecake

The delicious cheesecake but mini!

Nothing is better than a good dessert to end a meal and what better than a simple cheesecake with the always beloved oreo biscuits? For this recipe, however, I chose to prepare convenient single portions to limit waste and to make the recipe a little more chic!

Ingredients and useful tools (4 servings)

Before starting to list the ingredients, it seems good and right to note that for the preparation, pastry cutters of 7.5cm in diameter and 4.5cm thick

    For the base:

  • Oreo biscuits 150 g
  • Butter 60 g

    • For the cream:

  • Fresh spreadable cheese 125 g
  • Icing sugar 35 g
  • Fresh liquid cream 100 g

Preparation

  1. Separate the biscuit from the cream inside. Then grind the biscuits with the softened butter in a blender until a mixture is obtained crumbled and well mixed. Then arrange your pastry cutters and fill them with the mixture, with the back of a spoon crush the mixture until to fill the bases well. Then place them in the fridge for 30 minutes
  2. Whip the cream with the icing sugar with a whisk. In another bowl, pour the oreo cream and the spreadable cheese and mix everything with the whisk for about 1 minute. Once you have obtained a smooth cream, add the cream.
  3. Then take the bases from the fridge and pour the cream inside. Use a spoon to level the surface.
  4. Now blend a few whole oreos and use the mixture to cover the cream
  5. Put the cheesecakes in the fridge for about 8 hours, I leave them overnight, and they will be ready to be enjoyed!
